Mango Juice Mania!

How to Play

  • This is an incremental clicker game.
  • Click the "Sell Mango Juice!" button to make and sell mango juice.
  • When you make enough money, you can purchase items to auto-click mango juice.
  • The more items you buy, the faster you go.
  • Beware! When you you buy an item, the price increases for that item!
